Safety Tips

  • Take swimming lessons at your local center. The YMCA hosts swimming lessons for example.
  • Children between the ages of 1-4 are the most susceptilbe victims to drowning. Close supervision of children is advised.
  • Try to go swimming obly when there are lifeguards on duty.
  • Avoid alcoholic beverages around large bodies of water. Alcohol consumption can lead to unintentional injuries or even death.
  • Wear protective gear such as life jackets when participating in water-related activities.
